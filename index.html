Little Story

This is a story of a wife who's only aim is to cook a nutritional food for her family. She learn to cook of her own. Tried and failed many times of cooking which her children and husband finally crave for food she cooks.

Until that she fell in love in cooking she study the art of baking and coffee making as she turned to be a Baker and Barista. She started to make customized cakes and pastries and sell it on facebook market. It was so amazing that she had lots of customers and that time she sell not only cakes and pastries but also different kind of dishes online. This wife is not only a cook but also become a TESDA trainer. At this time her passion in cooking never stop by any reason, that is why she open this site that will bring her passion to many people.
